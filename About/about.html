We Are

Seaside

The Luxury Experience
You'll Remember

Welcome to our luxurious hotel, where sophistication, impeccable service, and unparalleled comfort await you. From the moment you step into our grand lobby, you'll be immersed in an atmosphere of opulence and refined elegance. As you enter our elegant establishment, you will be greeted by a captivating ambiance that exudes sophistication and tranquility.

Hotel Facilities
Restaurant
Embark on a culinary journey at our exquisite restaurant. Our expert chefs craft delectable dishes using the finest ingredients, offering a dining experience that delights the senses and satisfies the most discerning palates. From innovative cuisine to classic favorites, every meal is a celebration of flavor and artistry..
Fitness Area
Maintain your wellness routine in our state-of-the-art fitness center. Equipped with the latest cardio and strength training machines, our gym provides the perfect environment for invigorating workouts. Whether you're a fitness enthusiast or just looking to stay active during your stay, our facilities cater to all levels of exercise needs.
Mini Bar
Indulge in convenience with our thoughtfully stocked mini bar. Offering a curated selection of premium beverages and gourmet snacks, it's perfect for a quick refreshment or a late-night treat. Enjoy the luxury of in-room indulgence at your fingertips.
Meeting Room
Host successful business gatherings in our sophisticated meeting spaces. Equipped with cutting-edge technology and versatile layouts, our rooms adapt to various event needs. From intimate brainstorming sessions to large conferences, we provide the ideal setting for productive and impressive corporate events.
Laundry Service
Experience the convenience of our premium laundry service. Our attentive staff ensures your garments receive expert care, returning them fresh, crisp, and ready to wear. Enjoy the luxury of impeccably clean clothes without lifting a finger, allowing you to focus on enjoying your stay.
Satelite TV
Stay connected and entertained with our extensive satellite TV offerings. Enjoy a wide array of international channels, the latest news, sports events, and premium movie selections. Our high-definition screens and quality sound systems ensure an immersive viewing experience from the comfort of your room.
Safe Box
Ensure the security of your valuables with our in-room safe boxes. Featuring advanced locking mechanisms and spacious interiors, our safes provide peace of mind for storing important documents, jewelry, and electronic devices. Rest easy knowing your possessions are protected throughout your stay.
Parking Area
Enjoy hassle-free parking in our secure and spacious parking area. With 24/7 surveillance and easy access to the hotel, your vehicle's safety is assured. Valet service is also available for added convenience, allowing you to step directly from your car into our welcoming lobby.
Swimming Pool
Dive into relaxation in our pristine swimming pool. Whether you're looking to swim laps or simply lounge poolside, our beautifully designed pool area offers a refreshing retreat. Surrounded by comfortable loungers and lush landscaping, it's the perfect spot to unwind and soak up the sun.
